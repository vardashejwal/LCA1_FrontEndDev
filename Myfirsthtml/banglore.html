5 Facts about Banglore

  1. Bangalore is the birthplace of the popular rava idli, which was invented during World War II.
  2. Bangalore is known as the Silicon Valley of India because it's a major hub for the IT sector, with headquarters of companies like Wipro, Infosys, Oracle, and Google.
  3. Bangalore is also known as the Garden City because of its tree-lined streets and pleasant weather.
  4. It's the third most populated city in India.
  5. It has the highest concentration of mosques, churches, and temples in India.

Places to visit in Banglore

  1. Bangalore Palace: A majestic palace reminiscent of Windsor Castle, offering a glimpse into the city's royal past.
  2. Cubbon Park: A haven of greenery in the city center, perfect for a leisurely stroll or a picnic.
